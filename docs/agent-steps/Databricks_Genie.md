
# Databricks Genie Setup Guide

## 1. Get the PAT Token

| Step | Description | Screenshot |
|------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | Open your Databricks workspace. | ![step-1.1](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-1.1.png) |
| 2 | Click on your **Profile** (top left corner). | ![step-1.2](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-1.2.png) |
| 3 | Click on **Settings**. | ![step-1.3](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-1.3.png) |
| 4 | Click on **Developer** under the User tab. | ![step-1.4](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-1.4.png) |
| 5 | Click on **Manage** for Access Token. | ![step-1.5](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-1.5.png) |
| 6 | Click on **Generate new token**. | ![step-1.6](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-1.6.png) |
| 7 | Provide a comment for the token, set the validity if required, and click **Generate**. | ![step-1.7](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-1.7.png) |
| 8 | Copy the token and click **Done**. | ![step-1.8](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-1.8.png) |

## 2. Get the Endpoint

| Step | Description | Screenshot |
|------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | Open your Databricks workspace. | ![step-2.1](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-2.1.png) |
| 2 | Copy the page URL (e.g., `https://xxx-xxxxx-xxxx.cloud.databricks.com/browse?o=xxxxxxxxxx`). | ![step-2.2](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-2.2.png) |
| 3 | Your Databricks endpoint is: `https://xxx-xxxxx-xxxx.cloud.databricks.com` | ![step-2.3](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-2.3.png) |

## 3. Get the Space ID

| Step | Description | Screenshot |
|------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | Click on the **Genie** tab (left sidebar). | ![step-3.1](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-3.1.png) |
| 2 | Click on the particular Genie. | ![step-3.2](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-3.2.png) |
| 3 | Click on **Configure** and go to the **Settings** tab. | ![step-3.3](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-3.3.png) |
| 4 | Copy your Genie Space ID. | ![step-3.4](../../screenshots/Databricks_Genie/step-3.4.png) |
